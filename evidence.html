Evidence and Conclusion

Surat At-Tawbah verse 29: Fight those who do not believe in Allah and the Last Day, nor comply with what Allah and His Messenger have forbidden, nor embrace the religion of truth from among those who were given the Scripture, until they pay the tax, willingly submitting, fully humbled.

This verse is referencing the fact that some of the muslim soldiers were running away from the battlefield due to their fear (which they overcame later). It was only revealed a few years after the battle of Hunain. This is a message to the people who fled the battle and to all people after them. It is a message which states to only fear Allah SWT. The treaty of hudaybiyyah was signed 2 years before the battle of hunayn (628-630 AD).

From this I learnt that you shouldn't run away from things and you should face them as you should fear things except for Allah. An example for the battle of hunayn is that if Allah has prescribed for you to die, you will die. It is all part of his divine decree. If you are unsure about something, just do istikhara as only Allah can change your decree for the better.
