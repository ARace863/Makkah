Fateh Makkah

Fateh makkah took place in 630 AD (8 years after hijrah). It can be translated to the muslim conquest of Makkah. The prophet SAW gathered 10,000 men after seeing a vision from Allah SWT in which he (SAW) was doing Hajj. The reason for Fateh Makkah was that the Prophet Muhammad SAW wanted to put an end to the injustice and oppression which occurred to the ummah surrounding the prophet and other Muslims in the area. It was a peaceful venture to Makkah al Mukarramah in order to take it back from the Quraysh and allow muslims to do Umrah or Hajj. It also marked the end of the wars between the Muslims and the Quraysh.
